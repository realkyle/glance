Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

A focused set of tools for getting instant AI answers without leaving your screen.

Instant Screen Capture

Press Ctrl+Shift+Space to capture the full screen, or Ctrl+Shift+D to drag a region. The window hides before the shot so it's never in the frame.

Streaming AI Responses

Answers stream word-by-word from Claude — no waiting for the full response. Powered by claude-sonnet-4-6 with full vision support.

Context Detection

Claude automatically identifies what's on your screen — Code, Browser, Document, Terminal, or Design — and tags the response with a colored badge.

Voice Input

Tap the mic button and speak your question. Audio is transcribed by OpenAI Whisper and dropped straight into the input — no typing required.

Conversation Memory

Every capture and follow-up builds on the last. Ask "why?" or "show me an example" and Claude remembers exactly what was on your screen.

Always on Top

Glance floats above all other windows, freely resizable, and collapses to a tiny pill when you need more space. Minimizes to the system tray, never the taskbar.

How it works

Three keystrokes to an answer

Glance stays out of your way until you need it. No switching apps, no copy-pasting, no context loss.

1
Capture your screen

Press Ctrl+Shift+Space for a full screenshot, or Ctrl+Shift+D to drag over any region. Glance hides itself so it never shows up in the image.

2
Ask your question

Type or speak a question before or after capturing. Skip the prompt entirely for an automatic context analysis — Glance will describe what it sees.

3
Get a streaming answer

Claude streams back a response word-by-word. Ask follow-ups naturally — every reply is in the same thread and Claude remembers the full history.
